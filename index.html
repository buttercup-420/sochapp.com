what soch does
Delivers a personalized Gita verse every day based on your calendar, weather, and mood.
A private journal to write your reflections, with color highlights to mark what matters.
Tracks your emotional history across moods and verses so you can see how you grow over time.
A home screen widget that keeps your verse with you throughout the day.
your data
Soch reads your calendar to understand your day and choose a verse that fits. Calendar data is processed in the moment and never stored.
Your journal entries are stored securely in Firebase and are only accessible to you.
Soch does not sell, share, or monetize your personal data. Ever.
